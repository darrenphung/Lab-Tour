Lab 225B - PSS

The high voltage (HV) cage: It has a signle phase transformer with ratings of 240V/50 kV and 8 kVA, a 5 kVA variac and a multi-range Hipotronics voltage divider. It can conduct air discharge experiments for various types of electrodes.

Lab225B is power system laboratory in school of EIE. It has a million dollar equipment - Power System Simulator (PSS), which is the only one in Australian universities. The advanced equipment covers every section in power systems, from power generation, distribution/transmission as well as loads. PSS can be used to study an individual electric apparatus, such as a synchronous generator, transmission lines/cables, transformers and static/dynamic loads. It also can be used to analyse and study networks with different configurations, as well as protection relays. It supports the teaching for a number of unit of study in the school.
