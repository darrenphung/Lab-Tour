Lab 435

Lab 435 is Energy Lab in School of EIE. It equipped with full range of Labvolt modeuls from power electronics , renewable enery to three-phase electrical machineries, such as IGBT inverter, Thyristor, rectifier, batteries, DC /induction/synchronous machines. It is also set up with advanced testing devices, from Tektronic scopes, multi-channel function generators, large rating DC power supply units, and Tektronic current probes. IT can be used to teach various unit of study offered for electrical engineering, power engineering and some research activites.
