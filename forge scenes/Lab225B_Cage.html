Lab 225B - Cage

Power System Simulator (PSS): the advanced equipment covers every section in power systems, from power generation, distribution/transmission as well as loads.

The high voltage (HV) cage in lab225B is used power engineering teaching, particuarly for high voltage engineering, in school of EIE. It has a signle phase transformer with ratings of 240V/50 kV and 8 kVA, a 5 kVA variac and a multi-range Hipotronics voltage divider. It can conduct air discharge experiments for various types of electrodes.
