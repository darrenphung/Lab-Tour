Tek MSO Oscilloscope offers 100 MHz bandwidth and 1 GS/s sample rate. With up to 18 channels for analyzing analog and digital signals, students and staff can quickly find and diagnose problems in complex designs. To capture long windows of signal activity while maintaining fine timing resolution, the device offer a deep record length of 1 Mpoints standard on all channels.

The function generator has dual-output channel and it can offer two reconfigurable testing RF signals to be investigated alongside with digital electronics and systems.

Digital Multimeter is used to measure common electrical variabls such as voltage (volts), current (amps) and resistance (ohms).

Benchtop triple output power supplies are used to power up electronics and electrical systems. Voltage outputs may be electrically connected for tracking. Each of the power supplies is equipped with independent controls for use.

Soldering Station (Hakko FX-888D/FA-400) is used to bond electronic components to PCBs.
